Campus Life

school library

The school has 2 librarys one in the PYP buildings and one in the MYP building. The library is the ideal place to get cozy and sit in a bean bag and read a book. In the MYP library there is a coffee machine too. What more could you want?

school Pool

The school pool is one of the most favorite things about neev. The pool has the maximum dept of 5 ft and boasts a heating system , diving boards and well managed equipments. It is located in a serene areas overlooked by plands and the school garden it really is a marvel.

school auditorium

Our school auditorium is where most of the assemblys, school plays and speeches were held. Our school auditorium is located in the MYP building and is huge. The auditorium has sourround sound speakers and state of the art lighting tech.

Inter house art competion
Inter house Art competition

In our school we had an inter house art competion. In this competion each house narmada , kaveri,and brumaputra houses have to present the best artwork to the juges. Who evers art work is really good wins about 50 points for their house. In this competition you have to show teamwork as well as skill to win this competition. This was a fun and enriching challenge which tested our skills and promoted creativity among students.

What's New

  • Online Learning

    While we Learn From Home and Work From Home, learning never stops, despite the given situation as Learning from home became the new normal in times of COVID-19, we saw this an opportunity to switch and turn to use the technology better by launching Distance Learning Opportunities for our students at Neev. Distance learning will serve as a platform in enabling the students to reach their highest potential and access their respective grade curriculum from the safety of their homes. In the global times of this pandemic, we take this opportunity to continue the process of learning when the school is closed for varied reasons (epidemic, political etc).
